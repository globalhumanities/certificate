Requirements

The certificate will be composed of four courses distributed as that
  • At least one core course (see Global learning for further description)
  • The other three courses are chosen from a list of elective courses provided by the Steering Committee each term. The selection criteria for the student are: 
      • At least one course must focus on a culture different from their own
      • The courses must be distributed in at least two of the following areas:
        • Global thought
        • Global imagination
        • Global Story and History-telling
