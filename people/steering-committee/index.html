Christopher Baker

baker_ch@auca.kg

Position: Visiting Faculty, American University of Central Asia (AUCA).

Academic Background: PhD in Theology and Religious Studies, University of Manchester; MA in Theology, University of Oxford; BA (Hons) in Philosophy and Theology, University of Oxford.

Research Fields: Intersections of religion, policy, and civil society; Social and environmental justice; Faith-based social action and partnerships; Critical urban theory, human geography, and resilience; Compassion, ethics, and public policy; Sustainable urban transitions and the role of religious communities in environmental change.
