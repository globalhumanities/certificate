Certificate’s Courses

In this section, you can consult the offering courses each term. 


Core courses

  • A core course in the Global Humanities provides foundational knowledge and critical perspectives necessary for understanding complex, interconnected human experiences and traditions. The purpose of these core courses is to equip students with the tools to analyze, interpret, and engage with the complexities of cultural borders, migrations, narratives, and artifacts. This cultivates a deep understanding of how diverse traditions, memories, and perspectives shape global and local human experiences.

Electives

  • The elective component of the Global Humanities Certificate invites students to explore diverse philosophies, artistic expressions, and stories from around the world, discovering how ideas, creativity, and narratives unite cultures and open new possibilities. Through Global Thought, Global Imagination, and Global Story- and History-telling, the students will engage with perspectives on identity, creativity, and memory, cultivating a critical and creative outlook for addressing contemporary challenges.
